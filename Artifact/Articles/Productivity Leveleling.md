## 1. Automatic File Organizer

>*Taming the Digital Jungle*

**Why it's useful:** Your Downloads folder: is it a treasure trove or a chaotic mess? If you're spending more time searching for files than actually using them, it's time for a change.

**What to build:** A Python script that acts as a meticulous librarian, automatically sorting files into their designated homes based on type.

**Features to include:**

*   Intelligent categorization: Documents, images, videos – all sorted with ease.
*   Custom rules: Bills go to "Finance," vacation photos to "Memories" – you're in control.
*   Scheduled sweeps: Keep things tidy with automated runs at set times.
*   Optional notifications: Get a heads-up when your digital helpers are on the move.

**Potential Time Saved:** Imagine reclaiming 20-30 minutes each week. That's time for a coffee break, a creative brainstorm, or simply a moment of peace.

> *Your Digital Safety Net*

**Why it's useful:** Data loss is a nightmare scenario. Protect yourself with a reliable, automated backup system.

**What to build:** A Python script that acts as your digital guardian, securing your important files on a regular schedule.

**Features to include:**

*   Selective file copying: Prioritizes the most critical files.
